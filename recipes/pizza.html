Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Recipe

  1. Spread dough.
  2. Spread sauce on dough.
  3. Put cheese on sauce.
  4. Put pepperoni on cheese.
  5. Cook in oven at 500 degrees for 15 minutes.
